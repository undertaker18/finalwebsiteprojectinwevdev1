“Wisdom Based on the Truth is Priceless.” This is the tag line of our school, the La Verdad Chistian College. We, as students of this institution, created this website for the readers that are enthusiast about the story of our beloved teachers, as well as the students and all the staff of the school and other schools. This is to share each story that can inspire the readers. How a student has been successful in his or her studies, struggles and keeping to continue the journey, associated endurance with every step and love of teachers to share their knowledge with students who become their children within the school.

We, Jun, Earl and Melrose created this project and we hope that every reader will be inspired through this medium and send their stories too. Salute to all teachers, students and staff.
